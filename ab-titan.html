ACADIE-BATHURST TITAN

  • Scoreboard & Social Media Motion Graphics

In August of 2022 I was hired by the Titan as a freelancer to create a set of motion graphics for in-arena usage on their video boards as well as on social media. I was asked to create graphics for power plays, penalty kills, goals, and wins, as well as "Go Titan Go". Aside from adhering to their brand standards, I was given full creative control of the direction of the project. The only other requirement was that each graphic had to be bilingual, due to the Titan serving in a market with both French and English speaking fans.
