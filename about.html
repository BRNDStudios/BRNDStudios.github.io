About Me

My name is Brendan, and I'm a graphic designer based in Pittsburgh.

I began designing in 2013, when a friend of mine introduced me to a few apps I could use on my phone to edit photos of hockey players. I really enjoyed it, and kept on doing it, even after my other friends faded away from that fad. Along the way I also discovered a passion for sports logo and uniform design.

This combined passion for sports and design has already led me to work with clients such as the Montreal Force, Connecticut Whale, Acadie-Bathurst Titan, the Premier Hockey Federation/National Women's Hockey League, as well as my alma mater's Athletics and Men's Lacrosse programs.
