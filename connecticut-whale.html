The templates included game day, starting lineup, scores, and a postgame "stats snap".

Animated Graphics

For goals, power plays, penalty kills, and shutouts, an animated motion graphic was created. The composition was made from scratch, using stock footage for many of the elements. The 3-D Whale was another creation by Heartlent Group as part of a separate project. Each goal and shutout graphic is customized for each player on the team, though a generic version also exists in case of a mid-season acquisition who does not yet have their own version.
