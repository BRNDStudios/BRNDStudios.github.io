PHF - BUNDLING STATS

  • Social Media Sponsored Content & Motion Graphics

As a part of the PHF’s partnership with Geico, we were asked to create a number of sponsored social media posts. I previously had an idea for a social campaign to look at the stat splits of each team’s performance in each of their main uniforms (home, road, and alternate), and we thought it was a great fit for these partnered posts. Geico was on board with the idea, and so we went through with the campaign.
